About me


I am in my fourth year of training towards Diploma/MA in Humanistic and Integrative Psychotherapy at Bath Centre for Psychotherapy and Counselling (BCPC).

I have completed a Foundation Certificate in Counselling at Bristol City College in 2017.

I have been working as a trainee psychotherapist for the past two years.

In my practice I draw on several years experience of working in a mental health field, both in the NHS setting (inpatient and community) and voluntary sector. I have been working with clients who have been facing anxiety, depression, loss of hope and motivation, lack of self-worth, regulating emotions, panic attacks, relationship difficulties etc. Many of them have suffered childhood trauma and some developed personality disorders or serious mental illnesses.

Other training:

I graduated at University of Ljubljana (Slovenia), Faculty of Arts, in 2007 and I hold a BLib degree.
